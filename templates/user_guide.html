User Guide
Complete guide to using AI Crop Doctor effectively

Getting Started

What is AI Crop Doctor?

AI Crop Doctor is an advanced machine learning application that helps farmers and agricultural professionals identify plant diseases from photographs. Our AI model can detect various crop diseases with high accuracy and provide treatment recommendations.

Supported Crops & Diseases
Supported Crops:
  • Tomato
  • Potato
  • Apple
  • Corn
  • Wheat
  • Pepper
  • Grape
Common Diseases Detected:
  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Bacterial Spot
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Rust
  • Scab
  • Leaf Curl
System Requirements
  • Internet Connection: Required for AI processing
  • Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Image Format: JPG, PNG, GIF supported
  • File Size: Maximum 16MB per image
  • Resolution: Minimum 300x300 pixels recommended

How to Use

Step 1: Upload Plant Image

Drag & Drop or Click to Upload

  • Click the upload area in the left panel
  • Select a clear photo of the affected plant
  • Or drag and drop the image directly
  • Alternatively, try sample images from the homepage
Photo Tips: Ensure good lighting, focus on affected leaves, avoid blurry images
Step 2: Initial Analysis

After uploading, our AI will:

  1. Process your image (usually takes 5-10 seconds)
  2. Display the detected disease name
  3. Show confidence level (accuracy percentage)
  4. Present the analyzed image in the left panel
85% Confident High accuracy detection
Step 3: Detailed Diagnosis (Optional)

For more accurate treatment recommendations:

  1. Click "Get Detailed Diagnosis" button
  2. Answer 5 categories of questions:
    • Plant Symptoms: Leaf discoloration, wilting
    • Environmental: Weather, temperature
    • Treatment History: Fertilizers, pesticides used
    • Pest Observations: Insects, damage patterns
    • Plant Management: Watering, growth stage
  3. Submit for AI-generated comprehensive report
Step 4: Review Recommendations

The AI Agronomist Report includes:

  • Integrated Diagnosis: Combined AI + your observations
  • Organic Treatment: Natural remedies and practices
  • Chemical Treatment: Recommended products (India-specific)
  • Prevention Strategy: Long-term disease prevention
  • Monitoring Timeline: When to expect results

Understanding Results

Confidence Levels
80-100%: High Confidence

Very reliable detection. Proceed with recommended treatment.

60-79%: Moderate Confidence

Good detection. Consider expert consultation for confirmation.

Below 60%: Low Confidence

Uncertain detection. Consult local agricultural expert.

Disease Categories
Fungal Diseases:

Blight, rust, mildew, scab - often weather-related

Bacterial Diseases:

Spots, wilts, cankers - spread through water/wounds

Viral Diseases:

Mosaic, curl, yellowing - vector-transmitted

Treatment Timeline
  • Immediate (0-3 days): Emergency treatments, isolation
  • Short-term (1-2 weeks): Active treatment application
  • Medium-term (2-4 weeks): Monitoring and follow-up
  • Long-term (1+ months): Prevention and maintenance

Tips & Best Practices

Photography Tips
Good Photos:
  • Natural daylight (not direct sun)
  • Focus on affected leaves/areas
  • Multiple angles if possible
  • Clean, unobstructed view
  • Stable, non-blurry images
Avoid:
  • Very dark or bright lighting
  • Blurry or out-of-focus shots
  • Too much background clutter
  • Screenshots from other sources
  • Heavily filtered images
Maximizing AI Accuracy
  1. Multiple Samples: Upload 2-3 different affected leaves
  2. Complete Questions: Answer all diagnostic questions thoroughly
  3. Recent Photos: Use current images, not old ones
  4. Context Information: Provide detailed environmental conditions
  5. Cross-Reference: Compare with similar cases in history
Disease Prevention
Proper Watering

Avoid overhead watering, water at soil level

Air Circulation

Ensure adequate spacing between plants

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops to break disease cycles

Garden Hygiene

Remove infected plant debris promptly

When to Consult Experts
  • Confidence level below 60%
  • Disease spreading rapidly despite treatment
  • Multiple diseases detected simultaneously
  • Valuable/commercial crop at risk
  • Unfamiliar or severe symptoms

Troubleshooting

Common Issues
Problem: "File upload failed"
Solutions:
  • Check file size (max 16MB)
  • Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF)
  • Try a different image
  • Check internet connection
Problem: "Low confidence results"
Solutions:
  • Upload clearer, better-lit photos
  • Focus on clearly affected areas
  • Try multiple angles
  • Ensure disease symptoms are visible
Problem: "Analysis takes too long"
Solutions:
  • Check internet connection speed
  • Reduce image file size
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try during off-peak hours
FAQ
Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, images are processed securely and not stored permanently on our servers.

Q: Can I use this offline?

A: No, internet connection is required for AI processing.

Q: How accurate is the detection?

A: Our model achieves 95%+ accuracy on tested crops and diseases.

Q: Can I save my results?

A: Yes, all analyses are automatically saved in your History section.
